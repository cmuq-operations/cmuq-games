Room 1: The Inbox of Doom

Your inbox has exploded. Somewhere in these emails, a secret code is hidden. Click each email and look for the highlighted letters — unscramble them to find the password.

The highlighted letters spell a word related to what everyone at CMU-Q desperately needs. Read them in order from top to bottom.
Room 2: The Headline Hustle

It's hard to tell what's real anymore. Classify each headline as REAL or FAKE. You need at least 6 out of 8 correct to move on.

The CMU-Q specific ones are almost all fake. Real headlines tend to be the surprising global/science facts.
